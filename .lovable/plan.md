

# Stream Net Mirror — Netflix-Style Streaming Landing & Payment App

## Overview
A sleek, dark-themed streaming platform called **Stream Net Mirror** that showcases OTT content access, processes a one-time ZMW 39 payment via Mobile Money, and redirects users to the official NetMirror streaming portal. Uses the hexagon "S" logo as primary and red "N" as favicon.

## Pages & Features

### 1. Landing Page (Home)
- **Hero Section**: Full-width cinematic banner with gradient overlay, headline "Unlimited Movies, TV Shows & More", subtext "Access Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, HBO Max & 50+ OTT platforms", email input + "Get Started" CTA button
- **Trending Now**: Horizontal scrollable carousel of movie/show cards with numbered ranking (Netflix-style Top 10)
- **OTT Partners Section**: Grid showcasing supported platforms (Netflix, Disney+, Prime Video, HBO Max, Hulu, Peacock, Paramount+, Apple TV+, YouTube) with their logos/icons
- **Features Grid**: 4 cards — "Ad-Free Streaming", "HD & 4K Quality", "Multi-Device Access", "35+ Languages"
- **"More Reasons to Join"**: Cards similar to Netflix (Watch on TV, Download shows, Watch everywhere, Kids profiles)
- **How It Works**: 3-step flow — Sign Up → Pay ZMW 39 → Get Instant Access
- **App Download Section**: Play Store download button, QR code placeholder, web streaming option
- **FAQ Accordion**: 6-8 common questions

### 2. Payment Page
- Clean payment form: Name, Phone Number, select Mobile Money provider (Airtel Money / MTN MoMo)
- Single payment of **ZMW 39** clearly displayed
- Mock payment flow — shows processing → success screen
- On success: redirect message with link to NetMirror streaming portal
- Fine print about refunds being available

### 3. Account/Access Page (Post-Payment)
- Confirmation with access credentials/link to the NetMirror platform
- Quick links to supported OTT content
- Download app instructions (Android APK, iOS WebView)

### 4. Footer (All Pages)
- **Disclaimer**: The full educational/informational disclaimer text provided
- **Terms & Conditions**: Chargeback policy, refund availability (in fine print), usage terms
- **Privacy Policy**: Data handling, cookies, third-party links
- **DMCA Notice**: Copyright compliance statement
- **Contact**: onlineplagiarismremover@gmail.com
- Footer links grid (FAQ, Help Center, Account, etc.)
- Language selector, copyright notice
